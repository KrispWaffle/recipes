Cookie Recipe

Description

A cookie is a sweet baked treat that is typically small, round, and flat. It is made from a simple dough composed of ingredients such as flour, sugar, butter, and eggs, with various flavorings and mix-ins added to create different types of cookies. Cookies can be soft and chewy, crisp and crunchy, or somewhere in between, depending on the ingredients and baking time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Optional mix-ins like chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruit

Instructions

  1. Cream together softened butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs and any flavorings, such as vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until dough forms.
  5. Add chocolate chips, nuts, or other mix-ins if desired.
  6. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven according to recipe instructions.
  8. Cool cookies on baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  9. Enjoy your homemade cookies!
